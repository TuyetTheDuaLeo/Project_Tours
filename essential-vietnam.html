Trip Highlights:

You are uniquely given a chance to witness the fertile and lush flood plains of Mekong Delta and the splendor of Vietnam’s most renowned cities including Hanoi, Hue, Hoian and Saigon. The tour is also an exotic experience with generous views of the world’s famous natural wonder – Halong Bay.

  • See the richness of Mekong Delta
  • Enjoy the splendor of Halong Bay and wonderful beach vacation in Mui Ne
  • Understand more about Vietnamese history with trip to Cu Chi Tunnels
